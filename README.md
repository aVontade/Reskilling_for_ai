# Reskilling for the AI Economy

**How Organizations and Individuals Can Thrive Through Learning, Unlearning, and Relearning**

---

## 📖 About This Book

The AI revolution is here. Jobs are being transformed, industries disrupted, and skills made obsolete faster than ever. But this isn't a story of replacement — it's a story of **amplification**.

*Reskilling for the AI Economy* is a practical guide for anyone who wants to thrive in the age of AI — whether you're an individual navigating your career, a leader building an AI-ready workforce, or a policymaker shaping the future of work.

**Author:** Hamede Ali
